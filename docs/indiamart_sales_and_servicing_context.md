# indiamart_sales_and_servicing_context.md

## Purpose

This document explains the IndiaMART sales and servicing context relevant to calling sellers. It focuses on why IndiaMART sales/service teams may contact sellers, what objectives a call may have, what seller pain points matter, and how internal-observed terms should be understood without inventing unsupported definitions.

## Source-quality note

- Public facts come from IndiaMART investor material, app listings, and public product/proposal references.
- Attachment-derived facts come from user-provided IndiaMART product and servicing decks.
- Several terms are internal-observed and are not publicly defined in reliable sources. These are marked clearly.

## Why IndiaMART sales or servicing teams may contact sellers

From the user-provided product and servicing decks, IndiaMART sales/service calls may be tied to:

| Call reason | IndiaMART-specific context |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | Helping a supplier create account, verify details, add products, install app, start using Seller.IndiaMART.com and Lead Manager. |
| Catalogue setup | Adding product names, images, product groups, categories, and business information. |
| BuyLead activation | Explaining BuyLead balance, searching leads, filters, shortlist, and consuming fresh leads. |
| Lead handling | Helping seller reply, call, email, label, set reminders, send quotations, and follow up. |
| Paid subscription discussion | Explaining MDC, TrustSEAL, Maximiser, Star, Leader, Industry Leader, IM Insta, export packages, or upgrades. |
| Renewal | Ensuring seller understands value from IndiaMART services and renews before end date. |
| Upsell | Suggesting higher visibility, trust, communication, or export services based on seller context. |
| Service support | Resolving issues around leads, listing, catalogue, PNS, payments, Work Order, profile, or product quality. |
| Payment/package discussion | Guiding seller on pay.indiamart.com, cheque/DD, NEFT/net banking, NACH, annual/monthly/multi-year plans. |
| Complaint handling | Listening to seller concerns around lead quality, missed calls, conversion, visibility, wrong location, wrong category, or support experience. |

Sources: User-provided `Marketplace Overview V1.0`, slides 15 to 18; `MDC_3.0`, slides 18 to 22; `Product Overview & Servicing`, slides 10 to 18.

## Publicly inferable call objectives

The exact internal sales script is not public. However, based on IndiaMART's public business model and user-provided training, the following objectives are reasonable and IndiaMART-specific:

| Objective | What the salesperson may need to know before calling |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | Seller business type, contact verification, GST, app installation, product list, catalogue status. |
| Subscription sale | Current plan, seller goals, category competition, lead appetite, trust needs, budget, renewal date. |
| Renewal | Current usage, leads received, lead consumption, callbacks, catalogue updates, service issues, satisfaction. |
| Upsell | Whether seller wants higher visibility, more BuyLeads, TrustSEAL credibility, IM Insta WhatsApp response, or export leads. |
| Lead issue resolution | Direct enquiries vs BuyLeads vs calls vs catalogue views, fresh lead usage, filters, and response time. |
| Catalogue improvement | Product name, image, price, category, city, specification, and duplicate/incorrect product checks. |
| Payment discussion | Work Order status, invoice/proforma context, payment mode, NACH/monthly/annual plan, GST. |
| Service follow-up | Whether promised actions were completed and whether seller is satisfied. |

## Seller pain points likely relevant to IndiaMART services

| Seller pain point | IndiaMART-specific diagnostic areas |
|---|---|
| "I am not getting leads." | Check package type, category demand, listing visibility, product quality, BuyLead balance, lead alerts, and seller search/filter usage. |
| "Leads are not converting." | Check response speed, buyer profile, TrustSEAL, price competitiveness, quotations, follow-up, and catalogue clarity. |
| "I get wrong leads." | Check category mapping, product names, preferred locations, buyer requirements, and seller consumption filters. |
| "Buyers do not pick calls." | Check time-to-call, number route, PNS, WhatsApp/IM Insta, email/Lead Manager follow-up. |
| "I miss calls." | Check PNS setup, number verification, simultaneous ring, call availability, and callback process. |
| "My listing is not visible." | Distinguish search relevance from listing order. Check paid status, MCAT, image, price, catalogue enrichment, and location. |
| "My catalogue is poor." | Check MDC/Maximiser deliverables, product addition limits, product groups, images, template/domain needs. |
| "Buyers do not trust us." | Check TrustSEAL eligibility, business verification, certifications, profile completeness, and product proof. |
| "I need more buyers from other cities." | Check preferred locations, district logic, Star/Leader variants, and whether seller can serve those locations. |
| "I want international buyers." | Check export readiness, IE Code where applicable, and export service/foreign BuyLead context. |
| "Support is slow." | Follow Product Overview servicing principles: listen, own the issue, communicate next steps, follow through. |

## Servicing principles from user-provided training

The Product Overview & Servicing deck is directly relevant to sales/service calls. It emphasizes that service experience affects renewal and upsell.

| Servicing principle | Meaning in seller-call context |
|---|---|
| Ownership | Take personal responsibility for solving the customer issue. |
| Accountability | Do not pass the seller around without clarity. |
| Problem solving | Understand the exact reason the problem occurred and work toward a fit solution. |
| Active communication | Set expectations and keep seller informed about status and next steps. |
| Follow-through | Confirm that the seller is satisfied after the solution. |
| Listening | Avoid assuming the issue is the same as every previous complaint. |
| Tone | Customer should feel heard, not dismissed. |

Sources: User-provided `Product Overview & Servicing [Autosaved]`, slides 12 to 18.

## Call-preparation fields that are IndiaMART-specific

A seller-call-preparation system should use IndiaMART business context such as:

| Field | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Current package | Determines what seller has paid for and what benefits/limits apply. |
| Renewal date | Renewal calls depend on remaining tenure and current service value. |
| Catalogue type | Free, MDC, Maximiser, TrustSEAL, Star/Leader context changes call approach. |
| BuyLead balance | Helps discuss lead access and usage. |
| BL consumption history | Helps diagnose whether seller is using available demand. |
| Direct enquiries | Shows organic buyer interest from listing/catalogue. |
| Calls/PNS | Shows phone-based buyer contact and missed-call issues. |
| Catalogue views/Bizfeed | Shows buyer interest that may need follow-up. |
| Product categories/MCATs | Determines relevance, competition, A-rank categories, and paid service fit. |
| Preferred districts/location | Relevant for Star/Leader and location-based seller demand. |
| Product enrichment | Price, image, specs, product names, and category mapping. |
| TrustSEAL status | Important for credibility and conversion complaints. |
| Lead Manager activity | Shows whether seller replies, labels, sets reminders, sends quotations, and follows up. |
| Complaints/service tickets | Important for tone, retention, and renewal. |
| Payment/work order status | Important for activation, hosting, plan changes, and support. |

## Work Order, Proforma/Invoice, and Client Ledger

These terms are common in IndiaMART payment/subscription operations, but public definitions in the reviewed IndiaMART sources were limited.

| Term | Status | Meaning for project context |
|---|---|---|
| Work Order | Attachment-derived. MDC deck says hosting TAT is 7 to 10 days from Work Order issue date. It likely refers to the confirmed service order that triggers production/hosting work. |
| Proforma / Invoice | Internal-observed and standard business term. Likely used for seller payment documentation and GST billing. Verify exact IndiaMART workflow internally. |
| Client Ledger | Internal-observed and standard account term. Likely refers to customer account/billing ledger, payments, credits, renewals, and balances. Verify exact IndiaMART fields internally. |

Source: User-provided `MDC_3.0`, slide 21, plus user-provided business model image.

## Safe customer-facing wording

| Situation | Safe wording |
|---|---|
| Seller asks why leads did not convert | "Let us check how quickly leads were contacted, whether product details are clear, and whether follow-up/quotation was done from Lead Manager." |
| Seller asks why paid listing is not always first in search | "Listing order and search relevance can work differently. Let us review product relevance, images, price, category mapping, and buyer search terms." |
| Seller complains about low trust | "TrustSEAL can help showcase verified credentials and make it easier for buyers to identify your business as verified." |
| Seller wants more leads | "We should check your category demand, BuyLead usage, package, catalogue enrichment, preferred locations, and response process." |
| Seller wants export demand | "We should verify export readiness and whether export/foreign BuyLead services fit your business." |
| Seller has service issue | "I will first understand the issue fully, confirm the next step, and follow up after the fix." |
