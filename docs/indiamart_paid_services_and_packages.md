# indiamart_paid_services_and_packages.md

## Purpose

This document collects IndiaMART-specific context around paid seller services and packages. It focuses on seller subscriptions, catalog/listing services, TrustSEAL, BuyLead allocation, export/foreign leads, and internal-observed package terms relevant to seller calls.

## Source-quality note

- Public facts are taken from IndiaMART investor material, public app listings, and publicly indexed proposal/training PDFs hosted on imimg.
- Attachment-derived facts come from the user-provided IndiaMART product decks and business model image.
- Some terms are internal-observed and not clearly defined in public sources. They are marked as such.

## High-level monetization model

IndiaMART uses a freemium supplier model. Suppliers can create free listings/catalogues, while paid services offer additional visibility, trust, catalogue, communication, and BuyLead access.

Public investor material describes paid suppliers and subscription-style offerings around:

| Publicly described paid-service element | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Priority listing | Paid suppliers may receive enhanced positioning in listing-order contexts. |
| RFQ selection credits | Suppliers can use quota or credits to select RFQs/BuyLeads. |
| Lead Manager | CRM-like system for managing buyer enquiries and responses. |
| Web storefront | Seller catalogue or storefront presence. |
| Buyer profile | Additional buyer context for sellers. |
| Cloud telephony / PNS | Seller-side call routing and tracking capability. |

Public source: IndiaMART Q3 FY26 investor presentation.

## Catalog services

### Mini Dynamic Catalog / MDC

MDC is a paid catalogue service. User-provided material says MDC can include:

| MDC element | Attachment-derived detail |
|---|---|
| Catalogue type | Runs on subdomain and uses a single template design. |
| PNS | Preferred Number Service, where multiple supplier numbers can ring simultaneously. |
| BuyLead allocation | Weekly BuyLeads and daily bonus BuyLeads. |
| Higher listing | Higher listing than free supplier in IndiaMART marketplace. |
| Lead Manager | Seller can set reminders, add notes, add labels, send quotation, and filter by date. |
| Payment | User-provided MDC deck mentions monthly NACH, annual/multi-year, cheque/DD, NEFT/net banking, and pay.indiamart.com. |
| Hosting TAT | User-provided MDC deck mentions 7 to 10 days from Work Order issue date for MDC hosting. |
| Capacity | User-provided MDC deck says maximum 10,000 products in catalog, 100 products per product group, 100 product groups, and 100 products added by IndiaMART team before hosting. |

Sources: User-provided attachment, `MDC_3.0`, slides 4, 7 to 9, 12 to 13, 17 to 25.

Sales-call relevance:

| Seller situation | MDC relevance |
|---|---|
| Free seller wants structured presence | MDC gives paid catalogue and higher listing than free supplier. |
| Seller misses calls | PNS can route calls to multiple numbers. |
| Seller lacks lead discipline | Lead Manager tools help reminders, notes, labels, quotations, and filters. |
| Seller wants initial paid upgrade | MDC is a lower-level catalog service compared to Maximiser and higher listing-order products. |

### TrustSEAL

TrustSEAL is a verification and trust-signalling service. Public and user-provided materials describe it as reflecting verified business credentials on the seller's catalogue and IndiaMART directory listing.

Attachment-derived TrustSEAL elements:

| TrustSEAL element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Logo | Visible on paid catalog home page. |
| Stamp | Gives distinct visibility across IndiaMART platform. |
| Certificate | TrustSEAL certificate provided to seller. |
| Trust report | Verification/report element. |
| Verification | User-provided deck says VKYC is mandatory for sellers opting for TrustSEAL. |
| Benefits to buyers | Helps buyers identify verified suppliers and make more confident decisions. |
| Benefits to suppliers | Attracts buyers, improves trust, provides competitive advantage over non-certified suppliers, and supports conversion. |

Sources: User-provided `TrustSEAL_3.0`, slides 4 to 15; public TrustSEAL PDF hosted on imimg.

Sales-call relevance:

| Seller situation | TrustSEAL angle |
|---|---|
| Seller says buyers do not trust them | TrustSEAL can be positioned as credibility and verification. |
| Seller receives enquiries but complains about conversion | Trust signals may help buyer confidence. |
| Seller competes in a crowded category | TrustSEAL visibility and verified records can help differentiation. |

### Maximiser

Maximiser is described in user-provided and public proposal-style materials as a more complete online marketing/catalogue solution than MDC.

Attachment-derived Maximiser elements:

| Maximiser element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Positioning | "Complete Online Marketing Solution" with branding, trust, and visibility pillars. |
| Domain | Runs on own domain, unlike MDC which runs on subdomain. |
| Templates | Multiple designs and color combinations, selected through showroom.indiamart.com. |
| TrustSEAL | Complimentary TrustSEAL is included, unlike MDC. |
| Corporate email | Includes corporate email IDs in user-provided Maximiser comparison. |
| BuyLead allocation | Higher allocation than MDC in the user-provided Maximiser deck. |
| PNS | Offered to all Maximiser suppliers. |
| Prospect | Seller seeking higher listing, authenticity, trustworthy image, professional image, own domain/corporate email, colorful templates, more enquiries and leads. |

Sources: User-provided `Maximiser_3.0`, slides 3, 6 to 12; public Maximiser PDF hosted on imimg.

Sales-call relevance:

| Seller situation | Maximiser angle |
|---|---|
| Seller wants professional brand image | Own domain, templates, corporate email, video/PDF brochure references. |
| Seller wants trust plus catalogue | Maximiser includes TrustSEAL in training context. |
| Seller wants more enquiries than MDC | Higher listing and higher BuyLead allocation are relevant talking points. |

## IM Insta

IM Insta is a WhatsApp integration product described in the user-provided IM Insta deck.

| IM Insta element | Attachment-derived detail |
|---|---|
| Purpose | Integrating WhatsApp with IndiaMART. |
| Buyer-initiated flow | Buyer clicks WhatsApp icon on m-site/app, conversation appears in LMS, seller replies via LMS, buyer receives message on WhatsApp. |
| Seller-initiated flow | Supplier consumes a BuyLead or receives enquiry in LMS and sends pre-crafted WhatsApp templates. |
| Buyer response benefit | Deck claims up to 3X increase in buyer responses. |
| Eligible products | Deck lists IM Insta Gold, Platinum, and Diamond tied to TrustSEAL/Maximiser/IVE, Star products, and Leader products. |
| Pricing observed | Annual prices shown as Rs. 15,000, Rs. 30,000, and Rs. 40,000 excluding GST. |
| Restrictions | Some industries are restricted. Templates cannot be customized by seller because they are Meta-approved. |
| STOP rule | Buyer can reply STOP, muting seller for 90 days. |

Source: User-provided attachment, `IM Insta PPT + FAQ`, slides 5 to 7, 10, 16, 26, 28 to 31.

## How paid services may relate to seller outcomes

| Outcome area | Paid-service relation |
|---|---|
| Visibility | Star, Leader, Industry Leader, TrustSEAL, Maximiser, and MDC are positioned around improved listing or visibility compared with lower tiers. |
| Trust | TrustSEAL and Maximiser's complimentary TrustSEAL can help sellers demonstrate verified credentials. |
| Lead access | BuyLead balance/credits and weekly/daily allocations determine how many BuyLeads sellers can consume without buying each separately. |
| Catalogue quality | MDC/Maximiser provide structured catalogue presence and product addition/enrichment support. |
| Response speed | Lead Manager, PNS, IM Insta, notifications, and alerts can help seller response and follow-up. |
| Renewal value | Product Overview & Servicing deck says service plus product leads to customer satisfaction and better renewals. |
| Conversion | Better visibility and trust can support conversion, but no service should be treated as a guaranteed-order product unless contractually stated. |
