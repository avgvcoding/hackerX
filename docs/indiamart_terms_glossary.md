# indiamart_terms_glossary.md

## Purpose

This glossary defines IndiaMART and domain terms useful for a sales-call-preparation project. Each term includes meaning, source status, source reference, and why it matters for seller calls.

## Source-status legend

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Public | The term or concept is visible in public IndiaMART material, app listings, investor material, or public product/proposal pages. |
| Attachment-derived | The term is defined or used in user-provided IndiaMART decks or image. |
| Internal-observed | The term appears in user context or attachments but no reliable public definition was found in the reviewed sources. |
| Inferred | Meaning is a reasonable operational interpretation, not a confirmed IndiaMART definition. |

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning | Status | Source if available | Why it matters for seller calls |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Buyer | A business user who searches for products/services, contacts suppliers, or posts a buying requirement on IndiaMART. | Public, attachment-derived | IndiaMART investor presentation; Marketplace deck slides 3, 7 to 13 | Seller calls revolve around whether the seller is getting and converting buyer demand. |
| Seller / Supplier | A business that lists products/services on IndiaMART to receive enquiries, calls, BuyLeads, and visibility. | Public, attachment-derived | IndiaMART app listings; Marketplace deck slides 15 to 18 | Core customer for salespeople and paid services. |
| Enquiry | A buyer contact or requirement sent to a seller through IndiaMART surfaces such as Contact Supplier, Get Best Price, or requirement forms. | Public concept, attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slides 9, 12 to 13; BuyLeads deck slide 3 | Sellers evaluate IndiaMART value by enquiry volume, relevance, and conversion. |
| BuyLead / BL | Buyer requirement generated when buyer fills requirement details. Suppliers consume BuyLeads using BuyLead balance or paid lead access. | Public concept, attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slides 4, 8 to 10, 20 to 23; investor RFQ/BuyLead material | Central to lead generation, lead quality discussions, and paid package value. |
| Direct Enquiry | Buyer directly contacts or sends enquiry to a specific supplier through product/catalogue/contact flow. | Inferred from public/attachment flows | Marketplace deck slides 9, 12; BuyLeads deck slide 3 | Distinguishes seller-owned inbound interest from marketplace BuyLeads. |
| Callback | A return call or callback action between supplier and buyer. Star/Leader training lists callback to buyer in logic signals. | Attachment-derived, internal-observed | IM Star Pro - IM Leader Pro slide 19 | Useful signal for seller responsiveness and lead handling. |
| Catalog / Catalogue | Seller's structured company and product presence on IndiaMART. Can be free or paid, such as MDC or Maximiser. | Public concept, attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slides 16 to 18; Product Overview slides 5 to 7; MDC deck | The catalogue is the seller's core marketplace asset. |
| Product Listing | Individual product/service listing with product name, image, price, specs, city/location, and supplier details. | Public concept, inferred | Investor material on product specs, prices, photos/videos; Marketplace deck slide 26 | Quality of listings affects discovery, relevance, and buyer trust. |
| Category / MCAT | Marketplace category/category node used for mapping products, listings, lead relevance, and paid service configuration. Exact public expansion of MCAT not found. | Internal-observed, attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slides 25 to 27; Star/Leader deck slides 4 to 8, 19 to 24 | Wrong category can create poor visibility and irrelevant leads. |
| TrustSEAL | IndiaMART trust/verification service with TrustSEAL logo, stamp, certificate, and trust report. It indicates verified credentials and helps buyers distinguish verified suppliers. | Public, attachment-derived | TrustSEAL deck slides 4 to 15; public imimg TrustSEAL PDF | Important for sellers complaining about credibility or conversion. |
| STAR / IM Star | Listing order/higher visibility service. Star Pro training describes higher visibility, multi-location presence, categories, preferred districts, and Star Supplier icon. | Attachment-derived, secondary-public | IM Star Pro - IM Leader Pro slides 3 to 24; public proposal-style PDFs | Relevant upsell for sellers seeking higher category/location visibility. |
| PRIME | Public trademark/regulatory mention found as "INDIAMART PRIME", but no current product definition found in reviewed public or provided sources. | Internal-observed/historical public mention | IndiaMART RHP/trademark context found in public search; no product definition confirmed | Do not define customer-facing unless internal docs confirm. |
| IMA | IndiaMART Advantage Program, observed as a route for BuyLead balance and package-based BuyLead allocation. | Attachment-derived, internal-observed | BuyLeads deck slides 20 to 23; public proposal-style PDFs mention IndiaMART Advantage Program | Explains weekly/daily BuyLead credits attached to packages. |
| GLID | Likely internal unique account/company/buyer/seller identifier. Public definition not found. | Internal-observed, inferred | User request only; no public definition found | Salespeople may use it to fetch seller/account history. Verify internally before productizing. |
| STS | Internal-observed abbreviation. Public definition not found. | Internal-observed | User request only; no public definition found | Keep as internal field until confirmed. |
| DSR | Internal-observed. Could mean Daily Sales Report in sales operations generally, but IndiaMART-specific definition not found. | Internal-observed, inferred | User request only; no public definition found | May matter for salesperson reporting. Verify internally. |
| PNS | Preferred Number Service. MDC deck describes 5 supplier numbers ringing simultaneously. | Attachment-derived | MDC deck slide 4 | Helps reduce missed buyer calls and supports call tracking/routing. |
| Customer Voice | Internal-observed term. Public IndiaMART definition not found in reviewed sources. | Internal-observed | User request only | Likely seller feedback/sentiment/complaint context. Verify internally. |
| Lead Manager / LMS | Seller-side lead management system used to reply, label, filter, add notes, set reminders, send quotations, and manage WhatsApp-enabled conversations. | Public concept, attachment-derived | Apple App Store mentions Lead Manager System; Marketplace deck slide 18; MDC slide 9; IM Insta slides 5 to 6 | Main workflow for converting buyer enquiries into business. |
| Mini Dynamic Catalog / MDC | Paid catalogue service. Training says it runs on subdomain, has single template, includes PNS, BuyLead allocation, Lead Manager, and higher listing than free supplier. | Attachment-derived, secondary-public | MDC deck slides 3 to 25; public proposal-style PDFs | Entry-level paid catalogue product for online presence and lead support. |
| Maximiser | Paid online marketing/catalogue package with branding, trust, visibility, own domain, multiple template designs, PNS, and complimentary TrustSEAL in training context. | Attachment-derived, secondary-public | Maximiser deck slides 3 to 12; public Maximiser PDF | Relevant when seller wants professional brand image and stronger paid presence. |
| Export Leads / Foreign BuyLeads | Leads from foreign/international buyers or export-oriented packages. User-provided image lists Silver, Gold, Diamond, Platinum export tiers. | Attachment-derived, secondary-public | User-provided image; secondary Refrens package source | Relevant for exporters or sellers seeking international demand. |
| Work Order | Service order trigger. MDC deck says hosting TAT is 7 to 10 days from Work Order issue date. | Attachment-derived | MDC deck slide 21 | Important for activation, hosting, service delivery, and seller expectations. |
| Proforma / Invoice | Payment and billing documents. Public/user-provided source does not define exact IndiaMART flow in reviewed material. | Internal-observed, standard business term | User request; general business context | Relevant for subscription/payment/package calls. Verify workflow internally. |
| Client Ledger | Account ledger for client billing/payment/credits, likely internal. Exact IndiaMART public definition not found. | Internal-observed, inferred | User request only | Useful for payment history, dues, credits, adjustments, and renewals. Verify internally. |
| SPANCO | Sales pipeline framework. Public IndiaMART definition not found. General expansion often Suspect, Prospect, Approach/Analysis, Negotiation, Close, Order, but IndiaMART-specific usage must be verified. | Internal-observed, inferred | User request only | May classify seller sales stage in CRM or sales process. |
| Bizfeed | Generated when a buyer visits seller products or catalogue but does not post an enquiry. Consumed only by the seller whose product was viewed. | Attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slide 4 | Important for sellers who get views but not direct enquiries. |
| Catalogue Views | Buyer views seller product/catalogue pages. | Attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slides 3 and 15 | Useful interest signal for follow-up and product quality diagnosis. |
| Tender | Public-sector tender opportunities from Indian public sources available for sellers on IndiaMART, according to BuyLeads deck. | Attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slides 3, 18 to 19 | Useful for sellers seeking government/public procurement opportunities. |
| Preferred District | Location selected dynamically based on supplier BuyLead consumption behaviour in Star/Leader Pro context. | Attachment-derived | Star/Leader deck slides 7, 8, 19, 23 to 24 | Important for category-location visibility and seller geography discussions. |
| A-Rank MCAT | Category from which supplier is doing most transactions, used for Star/Leader category selection in training. | Attachment-derived | Star/Leader deck slides 4 to 6 | Helps decide which categories to attach to higher visibility products. |
| Premium Category | Category with higher marketplace value/volume indicators in Star/Leader training. The deck describes premium criteria involving SS/LS suppliers, total BL approved, AOV, and others. | Attachment-derived | Star/Leader deck slide 20 | Affects package pricing and pitch strategy. |
| AOV | Average Order Value. Star/Leader deck uses AOV while defining premium categories. | Attachment-derived, standard business term | Star/Leader deck slide 20 | Helps explain why some categories cost more or are more valuable. |
| QRF | Internal-observed signal. Star/Leader deck lists Positive QRF in transaction logic, but expansion not found. | Internal-observed | Star/Leader deck slide 19 | Verify internally before using. Could be a quality/requirement/feedback signal. |
| IM Insta | WhatsApp integration product where buyer/seller conversations are managed through LMS and WhatsApp. | Attachment-derived | IM Insta deck slides 5 to 7, 10, 16, 28 to 31 | Relevant for sellers who want faster buyer response and WhatsApp communication. |
| Daily Bonus BL | Daily bonus BuyLead allocation that expires the same day, according to BuyLeads/MDC decks. | Attachment-derived | MDC deck slide 7; BuyLeads deck slide 22 | Important for sellers to use lead balance before it lapses. |
| Weekly BuyLead Balance | Package-based weekly BuyLead balance allocated on Sunday and lapsing Saturday night, according to BuyLeads deck. | Attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slides 20 to 23 | Important for usage coaching and renewal value. |
| Hosting BL | BuyLead allocation associated with hosting, observed in the BuyLeads deck allocation table. | Attachment-derived | BuyLeads deck slide 22 | Relevant for package activation and early seller usage. |
| Seller.IndiaMART.com | Seller management surface for profile/product changes, buyer replies, BuyLead/tender purchase, labels, quotations, and settings. | Attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slide 18; BuyLeads deck slide 12 | Main seller account management location. |
| Pay.IndiaMART.com | Payment portal referenced for IndiaMART subscriptions. | Attachment-derived | MDC deck slides 20 to 21; Marketplace deck slides 23 to 24 | Relevant for payment/package activation conversations. |
| NACH | National Automated Clearing House. MDC deck mentions monthly NACH plan for MDC. | Attachment-derived, public banking term | MDC deck slide 12 | Relevant for monthly plan, mandate cancellation, and billing discussions. |
| Free Catalog Page / FCP | Free supplier catalogue page. Product Overview deck distinguishes free catalogue from MDC and Maximiser. | Attachment-derived | Product Overview slides 5 to 7; public proposal-style PDFs mention FCP | Important baseline before paid upgrade. |
| Listing Order | Supplier ordering/visibility hierarchy in IndiaMART directory contexts. | Attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slides 20 to 27 | Important to explain visibility products. |
| Search Relevancy | IndiaMART Search is based on relevancy and does not follow listing order exactly, according to training deck. | Attachment-derived | Marketplace deck slide 27 | Critical for managing seller expectations. |

## Term groups by seller-call use

### Lead and demand terms

| Term | Call use |
|---|---|
| Enquiry | Understand direct buyer contacts. |
| BuyLead | Understand paid/credit-based buyer requirements. |
| Direct Enquiry | Separate direct seller interest from shared marketplace demand. |
| Callback | Check seller follow-up discipline. |
| Bizfeed | Identify catalogue-view interest without enquiry. |
| Catalogue Views | Diagnose product/catalgoue interest. |
| Tender | Support sellers seeking public procurement opportunities. |

### Catalogue and discoverability terms

| Term | Call use |
|---|---|
| Catalog | Seller's main presence. |
| Product Listing | Unit of buyer discovery. |
| Category/MCAT | Relevance and package fit. |
| A-Rank MCAT | Best category for premium listing products. |
| Preferred District | Location fit for visibility services. |
| Listing Order | Paid visibility context. |
| Search Relevancy | Expectation management. |

### Paid-service terms

| Term | Call use |
|---|---|
| MDC | Entry paid catalogue and lead-support service. |
| TrustSEAL | Trust and conversion support. |
| Maximiser | Professional branding plus trust/visibility package. |
| Star | Higher visibility in selected categories/locations. |
| Leader | Higher tier than Star. |
| Industry Leader | Top category-city positioning context. |
| IM Insta | WhatsApp-based buyer response/communication. |
| Export Leads | International buyer demand. |
| IMA | BuyLead allocation/credits. |
